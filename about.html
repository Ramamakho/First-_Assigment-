About Us

Welcome to Bag Store, your first choice for everything related to bags. We are keen to provide you with the best bags, with a focus on quality and customer service.

Our Story

Bag Store was founded in 2020 by Zeina Daoud, and she had started her journey in a small workshop, when Zeina first started, her passion for handmade and eco-friendly bags led her to conduct a lot of research and progress in creativity and do more, so that Bag Store can provide the most distinctive, elegant and luxurious bags in the world. We now serve customers all over the world and are happy to be able to translate our passion into our website.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a variety of bags that meet the majority of needs and are not only stylish, but also functional.

What sets us apart

  • Quality: We only use the finest materials to make our bags, ensuring their durability and longevity.
  • Customer Service: We strive to provide exceptional customer service, ensuring that every customer is satisfied with their purchase.
  • Innovation: Here you will not see any imitation. We strive to come up with the strangest and most beautiful ideas and offer new designs and features for our bags to meet the changing needs of our customers.

Meet the Team

Our team consists of skilled craftsmen and designers And our customer service experts are all passionate about making the best bags and providing the best shopping experience.
